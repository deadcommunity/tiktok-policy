1 Overview

StoryReel ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting the privacy of our users ("you"). This Privacy Policy describes what personal data we collect when you use our platform, how it is used, and your rights.

We collect only the minimum data necessary to provide the Service. We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties for advertising purposes.

3 TikTok Data Access

When you connect your TikTok account through our OAuth 2.0 authorization flow, we request only the following scopes:

  • user.info.basic — read your public TikTok profile (username, avatar) to display in your dashboard
  • video.upload — upload video files to your TikTok account
  • video.publish — publish uploaded videos to your TikTok profile at your scheduled time

We do not access your TikTok messages, follower lists, analytics, or any scope beyond those listed above.

To revoke access: TikTok App → Settings & Privacy → Security → Manage app permissions → StoryReel → Disconnect.

4 How We Use Your Data

We use collected data exclusively to:

  • Authenticate you and manage your account
  • Process your story content and generate video files
  • Schedule and publish videos to your TikTok account at your chosen times
  • Automatically refresh expired access tokens to maintain uninterrupted publishing
  • Display your connected account information within your personal dashboard

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to delivering the Service.

5 Data We Do NOT Share

  • We do not sell or rent your personal data to any third party
  • We do not share your TikTok tokens or account credentials with other users or services
  • We do not use your content to train AI models
  • We do not transmit your data to advertising networks or analytics brokers

6 Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We implement the following security measures:

  • OAuth 2.0 with PKCE (S256) for all TikTok authorization — no passwords are ever stored
  • Access tokens are encrypted at rest and never exposed to other users
  • All API communication uses HTTPS/TLS
  • User content and tokens are isolated per account — no cross-user access is possible

7 Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account:

  • Your OAuth tokens are immediately invalidated and deleted
  • Your account data is removed within 30 days
  • Video content you have already published to TikTok remains on TikTok (managed by you via TikTok's platform)

8 Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction — request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion — request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Revocation — disconnect your TikTok account at any time via TikTok settings
  • Portability — request an export of your data

To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the details in Section 10.
