1 Overview

DeadCommunity ("we", "us", "our") operates a personal content automation platform. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how it is used, and your rights regarding that information.

We collect the minimum data necessary to operate the Service. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to any third party.

3 TikTok Data Access

When you authorize our application via TikTok's OAuth 2.0 flow, we request the following scopes:

  • user.info.basic — read your public TikTok profile (username, avatar)
  • video.upload — upload video files to your TikTok account
  • video.publish — publish uploaded videos directly to your profile

We do not request access to your TikTok DMs, follower list, private data, or any scope beyond what is listed above.

You can revoke access at any time: TikTok App → Settings & Privacy → Security → Manage app permissions.

4 How We Use Your Data

Data collected is used exclusively to:

  • Authenticate and authorize API requests on your behalf
  • Upload and publish video content to your TikTok account
  • Refresh expired access tokens automatically
  • Display the connected account name in the local application interface

We do not use your data for advertising, analytics profiling, or any purpose unrelated to operating the Service.

5 Data We Do NOT Share

We do not:

  • Sell or rent your personal data to third parties
  • Share your TikTok tokens or account data with any external service
  • Use your content for training AI models
  • Transmit your data to advertising networks
  • Store your data on cloud servers — all data remains on your local machine

6 Data Storage & Security

All sensitive data (OAuth tokens, API keys) is stored locally in a .env file on the user's own machine. We do not operate external servers or databases that hold your personal data.

Security measures include:

  • OAuth 2.0 with PKCE (S256) for all TikTok authorization flows
  • Tokens are stored only in the local environment file, not in source code
  • HTTPS used for all API communication
  • No third-party analytics or tracking scripts on this website

7 Data Retention

OAuth tokens are retained locally until you manually delete your .env file or revoke access through TikTok. We do not retain copies of your tokens on any server.

Published video files and metadata are stored locally in the application's output directories. You may delete them at any time.

8 Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access — request information about what data we hold
  • Deletion — request deletion of your data
  • Revocation — disconnect the app from TikTok at any time
  • Portability — export all local data (it is already on your own machine)

Since all data is stored locally on your own device, you have full control at all times without needing to contact us.
